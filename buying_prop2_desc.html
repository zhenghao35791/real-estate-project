Coburg Townhouse on Gilmore – close to train station & shops. Call for inspection

Architectural designed double storey townhouse with lots of nice features.

Open and spacious layout that brings in lots of light and comfort. It’s designed to have bedrooms downstairs and living/dinning/study upstairs.
